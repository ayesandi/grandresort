Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about your stay at Grand Vista Resort

Reservations

You can make a reservation through our website, by calling our reservations team at +1 (555) 123-4567, or by emailing reservations@grandvistaresort.com. Our online booking system is available 24/7 and offers real-time availability and pricing.

We offer flexible cancellation policies based on your booking type. For standard reservations, you can cancel up to 24 hours before check-in for a full refund. Non-refundable rates are available at a discount. Please check your specific booking terms for details.

Yes, we offer special rates for groups of 10 or more rooms. Group discounts vary based on the season and length of stay. Please contact our group sales team at groups@grandvistaresort.com for a personalized quote.

Yes, you can modify your reservation through our website or by contacting our reservations team. Changes are subject to availability and rate differences. Some restrictions may apply to non-refundable bookings.

Check-in & Check-out

Check-in time is 3:00 PM and check-out time is 11:00 AM. Early check-in and late check-out may be available upon request, subject to availability and additional charges. Please contact our front desk to arrange these services.

Early check-in and late check-out are available based on availability. Early check-in before 12:00 PM incurs a 50% room rate charge. Late check-out after 2:00 PM incurs a full room rate charge. Please request these services in advance.

Please bring a valid government-issued photo ID and the credit card used for the reservation. For international guests, a passport is required. We also recommend bringing your confirmation number for faster check-in.

Yes, we require a credit card deposit at check-in to cover incidental charges. The deposit amount varies by room type and length of stay, typically ranging from $100-$500 per night. The deposit is released upon check-out if no charges are incurred.

Amenities & Services

Your stay includes access to our fitness center, outdoor pools, private beach, tennis courts, and complimentary WiFi. We also provide daily housekeeping, concierge services, and complimentary airport shuttle service.

Yes, complimentary high-speed WiFi is available throughout the resort, including all guest rooms, public areas, and outdoor spaces. Premium WiFi with enhanced speed is available for business guests at an additional charge.

Yes, we have a world-class spa offering a full range of treatments and a state-of-the-art fitness center open 24/7. Spa services require advance booking. Personal training sessions and group fitness classes are available.

Complimentary valet parking is available for all guests. Self-parking is also available in our secure underground garage. Electric vehicle charging stations are provided at no additional charge.

Dining

We offer three restaurants: The Grand Bistro (fine dining), Ocean Breeze Grill (casual beachfront), and Sky Lounge (rooftop bar). Room service is available 24/7. We also have a coffee shop and poolside bar.

Yes, our chefs can accommodate various dietary restrictions including vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and food allergies. Please inform us of any dietary requirements when making your reservation or upon arrival.

Breakfast is included in our resort fee for all guests. We offer a full breakfast buffet at The Grand Bistro and continental breakfast options at our coffee shop. Room service breakfast is available for an additional charge.

Yes, we recommend making reservations for our restaurants, especially The Grand Bistro. You can make reservations through our concierge, online booking system, or by calling the restaurant directly.

Policies

We welcome pets in designated pet-friendly rooms for an additional fee of $75 per night. Pets must be under 50 pounds and well-behaved. A pet deposit is required, and pets must be leashed in public areas.

Grand Vista Resort is a non-smoking property. Smoking is only permitted in designated outdoor areas. A $250 cleaning fee will be charged for smoking in guest rooms or non-designated areas.

Guests must be at least 18 years old to check in. Guests under 18 must be accompanied by a parent or legal guardian. Photo identification is required for all guests at check-in.

We maintain a lost and found department for items left behind by guests. Items are held for 30 days. Please contact our front desk at +1 (555) 123-4567 to inquire about lost items. Shipping charges may apply for returned items.

Accessibility

Yes, Grand Vista Resort is fully accessible and ADA compliant. We offer accessible guest rooms, public areas, and facilities. All common areas are wheelchair accessible, and we provide accessible parking spaces and beach wheelchairs.

We offer accessible guest rooms with roll-in showers, grab bars, and lowered fixtures. Public areas feature wide doorways, accessible elevators, and ramps. We also provide TTY devices, visual alarms, and service animal accommodations.

Yes, our airport shuttle service includes wheelchair-accessible vehicles. We also provide accessible transportation for local attractions and can arrange for accessible taxis and car services upon request.

Our culinary team can accommodate various dietary needs and restrictions. Please inform us of any special dietary requirements when making your reservation, and we'll ensure your needs are met throughout your stay.

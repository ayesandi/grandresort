About Grand Vista Resort

Discover our story, values, and commitment to exceptional hospitality

Our Story

For over three decades, Grand Vista Resort has been a beacon of luxury hospitality, creating unforgettable experiences for guests from around the world.

Founded in 1990 by hospitality visionary Robert Grand, our resort began as a dream to create a destination where luxury meets nature in perfect harmony. What started as a small beachfront property has evolved into a world-class resort spanning 500 acres of pristine coastline.

Today, Grand Vista Resort stands as a testament to our founder's vision, offering unparalleled service, breathtaking accommodations, and experiences that create lasting memories for our guests and their families.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional hospitality experiences that exceed expectations, creating meaningful connections and lasting memories for every guest who walks through our doors.

Our Vision

To be the world's premier luxury resort destination, recognized for our commitment to sustainability, innovation, and the highest standards of hospitality excellence.

Our Values

Integrity, excellence, respect, and sustainability guide everything we do. We believe in creating positive impact for our guests, team members, and the communities we serve.

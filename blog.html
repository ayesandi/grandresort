Getting Here

Grand Vista Resort is easily accessible by air, land, and sea. The nearest international airport is just 30 minutes away, with complimentary shuttle service available for all guests.

  • By Air: Paradise International Airport (30 min drive)
  • By Car: Highway 101, Exit 45 (2 hours from downtown)
  • By Sea: Private marina with boat access
  • Shuttle Service: Complimentary airport transfers

Best Time to Visit

Our destination offers year-round appeal with each season bringing its own unique charm and activities.

Spring (March - May)

Mild temperatures, blooming gardens, perfect for outdoor activities

Summer (June - August)

Warm weather, beach activities, peak season with vibrant energy

Fall (September - November)

Pleasant weather, harvest festivals, ideal for cultural experiences

Winter (December - February)

Cooler temperatures, holiday celebrations, perfect for relaxation

Packing Tips

Pack smart for your stay with our comprehensive packing guide tailored to the season and activities you plan to enjoy.

Essentials

  • Comfortable walking shoes
  • Swimwear and beach cover-up
  • Sunglasses and sun hat
  • Lightweight clothing
  • Camera or smartphone

For Dining

  • Smart casual attire
  • Dress shoes
  • Light jacket or shawl
  • Accessories for evening wear

For Activities

  • Hiking boots (if planning outdoor activities)
  • Water shoes
  • Reusable water bottle
  • Backpack for day trips
